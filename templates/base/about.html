My Story

Born and raised in Nairobi, where I still reside. I am 24 years old, running an ambitious startups, while freelancing as a software developer. I am anticipating to graduate this year (2022) with a BSc in Actuarial Science.

Growing up my curiousity was my superpower, and it is still.... Like many other children in the Ghetto I lived a day at a time. Everyday's experience had nothing to do with ambitions nor passions (but fun, I'm glad it was that way). I techinically didn't know what my passion was, other than I wanted to be a judge ( for the enormous amount of power they yield, and also be a boss to my dad). Regardless, as time passed I quickly got obsesses with technology.

Tech being synonymous with games to me at the time. I would of course spare every change I had to spend it at popular play stations around the hood. Among the play station games of the late 2000s PES was my favourite, but I enjoyed Mortal Kombact 4 and Road Rash as well.

Education and Seeking a Career Path By Experimenting

An honest critic of my career ambitions can only tell of someone who was not sure of what path to take...

After passing my high school exams, the next chapter was getting into college. Since I didn't perform so good in English I couldn't pursue Law and because I was rejected for medicine (Yeap, sometime in high school I had decided I wanted to be doctor), I ended up pursuing Actuarial Science, in Daystar University. After learning it had maths, finance and some programming.

While at campus with my superpower (curiousity) and still being unsure of a career path, I spent a lot of time experimenting various career avenues. Significantly on online education platforms, such as Coursera, Udemy, YouTube and LinkedIn Learn. Well, before I realized I was on my way to be a Financial Analytic and got certified as a:

  • Graphics Designer for the students lead campus newspaper
  • Digital Marketer
  • Writing and Speech tutor

I also taught a few school workers some basic computer skills, and perhaps more noteable I was a Self-Taught Fullstack Software Engineer!

Ongoing Career Pursuits and Interests

With interest in Tech, Finance, Enterpreneurship, Education, Politics and History, Bitcoin seemed to incorporate all of them. So when I first heard of blockchain: the technology that powers the ecosystem of cryptocurrencies, I knew I wanted to join in early as a builder.

As alluded above I am a business owner and a freelancer. I opted for freelancing, hoping that I can have an humble time to create my personal projects and keep on learning with reduced responsibilities. Turns out I was wrong, freelancing has proven to be more challenging and demanding than I assumed. With freelancing you learn things the hard way! Despite, I'm keeping on with it.

Further Education and Ambitions

For the first time in my life, I feel certain with what I want to get into in the tech space. While always seeking my next tech gig, I'm keeping my eyes fixed at the prize while appreciating every experience on the path.

I hope to further my eductation to a masters degree and beyond, after I launch my blockchain base startup.
